Biography

I am a post-doctoral researcher with a background in computer science engineering and a master's degree in Networking and Communication. My passion lies in optimizing wireless networks, especially in challenging environments. My master's research focused on graceful degradation for tactical battlefield networks, while my Ph.D. work centered on AI- and non-AI-based optimization for 5G RedCap/5G-NR Light devices in Industrial IoT scenarios.

Looking ahead, my research focuses on Beyond-5G and 6G networks. I aim to explore sustainable and scalable network solutions to advance interconnected digital, physical, and human worlds.
